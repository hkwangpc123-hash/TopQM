2026 Hong Kong Workshop on Topology in Quantum Materials

April 21-24, 2026

Chinese University of Hong Kong
Shatin, N.T., Hong Kong




The workshop aims to convene leading researchers in the fields of topological magnetism and topological superconductivity for four days of focused discussion and collaborative exchange. By fostering dialogue between experimentalists and theorists, it seeks to advance the fundamental understanding of topological phases of matter, elucidate novel superconducting and magnetic orders, and explore their prospective applications in quantum technologies.
